- If GTN drops >50% from stake price, NS can claim compensation tokens
- Reduces downside risk for early NS partners

### Risk 2: Token Regulatory Scrutiny

**Scenario**: SEC classifies GTN as security despite utility focus
**Impact**: Legal costs $500K-1M, possible shutdown
**Probability**: LOW (15%) with proper structuring

**Mitigation**:
1. MIDAO structure (non-US entity, reduces SEC jurisdiction)
2. Clear utility: Stake-for-access = software license, not investment contract
3. NO public sale (only strategic private placement to accredited investors)
4. Howey Test analysis:
   - ✅ Investment of money: Yes (stake GTN)
   - ✅ Common enterprise: Yes (GTN platform)
   - ❌ Expectation of profit: NO (staking for ACCESS, not speculation)
   - ❌ Efforts of others: NO (NS uses platform themselves)
   - **Conclusion**: GTN is UTILITY token, not security

**Validation**: MIDAO structure used by 50+ DAOs without SEC issues

---

### Risk 3: Token Price Crash (Bear Market)

**Scenario**: GTN drops 80% from launch price (common in crypto)
**Impact**: NS partners see staked value drop, consider leaving
**Probability**: HIGH (60%) - crypto markets are volatile

**Mitigation**:
1. Long lock-up periods (1-3 years) = NS focused on utility, not price speculation

3. Messaging: "You're paying for platform access, token is payment method"
4. Staking rewards (Proof of Coordination) offset price drops

**Reframing**:

- Token upside is BONUS, not primary value prop

## Appendix: Token Mechanics Details

**Three Tier Staking Pools**:

- Tier 2: 10M GTN locked for 1 year
- Tier 3: 50M GTN locked for 3 years

**Access Control**:

- Members get platform access via wallet signature

**Early Unstaking Penalty**:
- Unstake before lock period ends = 30% penalty
- Penalized tokens → Treasury (not burned)
- Purpose: Discourage short-term speculation

- Phase 1 (Months 1-12): Advisory votes only

**Proposal Types**:
- **Standard Proposal**: 50% quorum, >50% approval

- **Emergency Action**: Founder veto (Months 1-24 only)

**Governance Scope**:
- Platform fees (5-10% range)

- Protocol upgrades

- Network incentive parameters
