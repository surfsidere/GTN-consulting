## Logistics & Mechanics

### Rotation Allocation System

**Hybrid Model** (Recommended):
- **70% First-Come-First-Served**: Global Citizens book available weeks (max 1 week/quarter)
- **20% Lottery**: Random assignment for members who couldn't book preferred times
- **10% Reputation-Based**: Reserved for high contributors (community participation weighted)

**Booking Platform Requirements**:
- Calendar showing available NS location slots across partner locations
- Booking system with fairness rules (1 week/quarter max per member)
- Waitlist and cancellation management (30-day cancellation notice required)
- Integration with NS location booking systems
- Automated reminders, confirmations, and feedback collection

---

### Coordination with NS Locations

**Quarterly Schedule**:
- GTN provides NS with 90-day advance schedule of incoming members
- Member profiles shared: Name, background, interests, contribution areas
- NS provides arrival instructions and community onboarding materials

**Communication Protocol**:
- Primary contact: GTN Community Manager ‚Üî NS Location Manager
- Weekly check-ins during pilot, monthly thereafter
- Real-time issue escalation protocol
- Quarterly partnership review meetings

**Member Tracking**:
- GTN members marked as "GTN Partnership" guests in NS system
- Usage data shared monthly: Attendance, participation, feedback
- Quality monitoring: Behavioral reports, contribution tracking

### Risk Mitigation

#### **Risk 1: Low Member Interest**
**Mitigation**:
- Survey interest before committing (validate 20+ interested members)
- Founding member pricing ($250/month) to drive early adoption
- Start small (10 members) and scale based on demand
- Ensure other Global Citizen benefits justify cost even without heavy NS usage

#### **Risk 2: NS Locations Decline**
**Mitigation**:
- Target NS locations with excess capacity (easier yes)
- Offer trial period with quality guarantee
- Provide value-add: Member contributions, programming, talent pipeline
- Alternative: Partner with high-quality coliving networks (Outsite, Selina, Cabin, etc.)

#### **Risk 3: Poor Member Experience**
**Mitigation**:
- Pre-visit orientation and expectations management
- Clear communication of community guidelines
- Rapid response to issues (24-hour escalation protocol)
- Satisfaction guarantee: Alternative location or partial refund if experience is poor

#### **Risk 4: Low Utilization (Wasted Investment)**
**Mitigation**:
- Cancellation policy: 30-day notice required or lose next quarter access
- Waitlist system: Open slots immediately go to waitlist
- Usage tracking: Adjust model if utilization <60%
- Incentives: Rewards/credits for members who actively use allocation

#### **Risk 5: Financial Unsustainability**
**Mitigation**:
- Reserve fund: 3-month buffer for NS membership payments
- Annual pricing lock: Negotiate stable pricing for 12 months
- Dynamic pricing: Transparent adjustment of Global Citizen fee if costs increase
- Exit clause: 90-day termination notice if model doesn't work
