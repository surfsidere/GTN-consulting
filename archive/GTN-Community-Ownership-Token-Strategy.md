# GTN Community Ownership Token Strategy

**Executive Summary**: DAO-led token raise achieving community ownership, founder governance retention, and philosophical alignment with GTN's transformation mission.

**Status**: Alternative capitalization strategy (Path B)
**Date**: January 2025
**Core Principle**: Community ownership > extractive capital

---

## The Core Question

**"Why give the investor role to extractive VCs when we could give it to the community we serve?"**

This is the fundamental tension:
- **Traditional VC Model**: Extractive investors demand 10-100x returns in 7-10 years through forced exits
- **Community Token Model**: Aligned stakeholders invest for long-term infrastructure, not quarterly profits

**GTN's Philosophy**: "You don't transform THEN coordinate. You transform BY coordinating."
**Capitalization Philosophy**: "You don't build THEN sell to community. You build WITH community ownership."

---

## Why Community Ownership Matters for GTN

### Philosophical Alignment

**GTN's Core Values**:
- Transformation through relationship (not extraction)
- Cooperative dynamics (not competitive pressure)
- Commons-based governance (not shareholder supremacy)
- Long-term consciousness evolution (not short-term profit maximization)

**Traditional VC Misalignment**:
- ❌ VCs need 10x returns → Pressure for aggressive monetization over mission
- ❌ 7-10 year exit timeline → Forces acquisition/IPO before transformation proven
- ❌ Board control → Product decisions driven by financials, not transformation impact
- ❌ Follow-on pressure → Must show hockey-stick growth, compromise values for metrics
- ❌ Exit requirement → Community loses ownership when GTN sold to acquirer

**Community Token Alignment**:
- ✅ DAOs invest for infrastructure → Success = GTN helps their coordination (not exit)
- ✅ No exit timeline → Can build for 20+ years if transformation requires it
- ✅ Governance participation → DAOs vote on platform direction, founders retain control
- ✅ Organic growth → Success = user transformation metrics, not just revenue
- ✅ Permanent ownership → Community NEVER loses ownership (tokens don't get acquired)

### Strategic Advantages

**1. Customer-Investor Alignment**

Traditional model: Customers pay you, investors own you (misaligned incentives)
Community model: Customers ARE investors (perfect alignment)

**For GTN**:
- 25 DAOs invest $100K each → They're both customers AND owners
- Their success depends on GTN succeeding (not flipping for 10x)
- They evangelize GTN to other DAOs (network effects from investors)
- They provide strategic value (partnerships, feedback, co-creation)

---

**2. Marketing Narrative**

**Traditional**: "VC-backed coordination tool" (sounds like every other SaaS)
**Community**: "Owned by the DAOs it serves" (unique positioning)

This narrative resonates with:
- DAO culture (decentralization, anti-extraction, commons)
- Network state philosophy (sovereignty, autonomy, emergence)
- Conscious communities (transformation over profit)

**Competitive Moat**: Discord/Slack/Notion are owned by VCs → GTN is owned by its users. This becomes a FEATURE in marketing.

---

**3. Governance Retention**

**Traditional VC Path**:
- Seed: 25% to VCs, founders 75% equity
- Dual-class shares: Founders 70% voting control (artificial mechanism)
- Series A: 15% more to VCs, founders 60% equity
- Board control: 2 founders, 2 investors, 1 independent (investors can outvote)
- Exit pressure: VCs push for acquisition when 7-10 year clock runs out

**DAO Token Path**:
- Token raise: 25% to DAOs, founders 60%, employees 15%
- Natural majority: Founders 60% voting control (no artificial dual-class needed)
- Series A: Optional (can remain token-funded if profitable)
- Governance: On-chain voting (transparent, no secret board pressure)
- No exit pressure: DAOs invested for long-term infrastructure, not exit

**Founders retain MORE control with token model** (60% absolute majority vs. 51% with dual-class equity).

**Traditional VC**:
- $2.5M from 3-5 VCs
- 2-4 month fundraising process
- Dilution: 25% equity given up
- Governance: Board seats, veto rights, protective provisions

**DAO Token**:
- $2.5M from 20-30 DAOs ($50-100K each)
- 2-4 month fundraising process (similar)
- Dilution: 25% tokens given up
- Governance: Proportional voting, on-chain transparency

**BUT**: Token model includes:

- $500-1K/month subscriptions from each (additional $180-360K ARR from investors alone)
- Network effects (investors evangelize to their DAO networks)
- Strategic value (partnerships, integrations, co-creation)

**Effective Cost of Capital**: VC = 25% equity for $2.5M. Token = 25% tokens for $2.5M + $180-360K ARR + network effects. Token is MORE capital efficient.

- Locks tokens into platform usage (reduces circulating supply)
- Investors can use tokens instead of cash for subscriptions

- If GTN becomes profitable, quarterly dividends distributed to token holders
- Example: $500K annual profit → 50% distributed ($250K) → $0.00025/token dividend
- DAO with 10M tokens earns $2,500/year in dividends (2.5% yield on $100K investment)
- Aligns investors with profitability (not just exit)

**4. Staking Rewards (Facilitator Economy)**
- Token holders can stake tokens to earn facilitation fees from Practice Pods
- Example: Stake 1M tokens → Earn 5% APY from facilitator fees (50K tokens/year)
- Creates demand for tokens (stakers remove from circulation)
- Connects token utility to platform usage (more Practice Pods = more staking rewards)

### Investment Terms (SAFT Structure)

**Instrument**: SAFT (Simple Agreement for Future Tokens)
**Compliance**: Reg D (accredited investors - DAOs qualify as institutional)

**Key Terms**:
- **Investment Amount**: $50-100K per DAO (minimum $50K)
- **Token Price**: $0.01/token ($10M FDV)
- **Tokens Received**: Investment amount ÷ $0.01 (e.g., $100K = 10M tokens)
- **Lockup**: None (tokens liquid immediately upon delivery)
- **Vesting**: None (DAOs receive all tokens at TGE)
- **Token Delivery**: 6 months post-close (SAFT compliance, platform launch)
- **Governance Rights**: Proportional voting (immediate upon token delivery)
- **Discount**: None (fair launch pricing for all DAOs)
- **MFN (Most Favored Nation)**: If better terms offered to later investors, early investors get same terms

**No Traditional VC Terms**:
- ❌ No board seats (governance via token voting only)
- ❌ No liquidation preference (tokens have no preference stack)
- ❌ No participation rights (no double-dipping on exits)
- ❌ No drag-along rights (founders control exit decisions)
- ❌ No veto rights (founders retain 60% majority)

### Token Utility Breakdown

**1. Governance Rights** (Immediate utility upon TGE)

**What Token Holders Vote On**:
- Platform feature roadmap (which patterns to prioritize)
- Pricing changes (increase/decrease subscription tiers)
- Treasury allocation (how to spend GTN treasury funds)
- Partnership approvals (which network states/DAOs to integrate with)
- Token economics changes (staking rewards, burn rate, revenue share %)

**What Token Holders CANNOT Vote On** (Founder reserved powers):
- Team hires/fires (operational control)
- Financial decisions <$100K (day-to-day operations)
- Product direction (founders set vision, community votes on execution)

**Governance Process**:
1. Proposal submitted on-chain (requires 1M tokens = 0.1% of supply to submit)
2. Discussion period (7 days, community debates in forum)
3. Voting period (7 days, token holders vote proportionally)
4. Execution (if >50% approval, proposal auto-executes via smart contract)

**Founders retain veto**: 600M tokens = 60% voting power → Can block any proposal

---

**2. Platform Access Credits** (Immediate utility upon TGE)

**Conversion Rate**: 1 token = $1 platform credit (pegged to USD, not floating)

**How It Works**:
- DAO subscription: $500/month → Pay with 50,000 tokens OR $500 cash
- Tokens burned upon usage (removed from circulation)
- Reduces circulating supply over time (creates scarcity)

**Example (DAO with 10M tokens from $100K investment)**:
- Year 1: Pay 600K tokens for $500/month subscription (12 months × 50K tokens)
- Year 2-16: Pay 600K tokens/year (10M tokens ÷ 600K = 16.6 years of platform access)
- After 16 years: All tokens used for platform access OR can buy more tokens on secondary market

**This creates real utility**: Tokens aren't just speculation, they're how you access the platform long-term.

---

**3. Revenue Share** (Activated when GTN becomes profitable)

**Profit Distribution Model**:
- 50% of net profit distributed quarterly to token holders
- 50% retained for treasury (platform development, reserves)

**Example Scenario** (Year 3, $1M net profit):
- Total distribution: $500K (50% of profit)
- Per token: $500K ÷ 1B tokens = $0.0005/token
- DAO with 10M tokens: 10M × $0.0005 = $5,000 quarterly dividend ($20K/year)
- Dividend yield: $20K annual ÷ $100K investment = 20% yield

**This aligns investors with profitability** (not just exit): DAOs benefit when GTN runs efficiently and generates profit.

---

**4. Staking Rewards** (Activated when Facilitator Economy launches)

**How Staking Works**:
- Token holders stake tokens in smart contract
- Staked tokens earn 5-10% APY from facilitator fees
- Facilitator economy: Realized citizens earn tokens by guiding Practice Pods
- 10% of facilitator earnings go to staking pool (redistributed to stakers)

**Example Scenario** (Year 2, 100 active facilitators earning $50K/year):
- Total facilitator earnings: 100 × $50K = $5M/year
- Staking pool allocation: 10% × $5M = $500K/year
- DAO stakes 10M tokens (1% of supply) → Earns 1% × $500K = $5,000/year (5% APY)

**This creates staking demand**: Token holders remove tokens from circulation to earn passive yield, reducing supply and increasing scarcity.

---

### Token Burn Mechanisms (Supply Reduction)

**Tokens are burned (permanently removed) when**:
1. Used for platform subscriptions (50K tokens/month per DAO → 600K tokens/year burned per customer)
2. Facilitator fees (10% of facilitation earnings burned to reduce inflation)
3. Governance proposals (1M tokens required to submit proposal → burned upon execution)

**Burn Rate Projection** (Year 3, 100 DAO customers):
- Subscription burns: 100 DAOs × 600K tokens = 60M tokens/year
- Facilitator burns: 100 facilitators × $50K × 10% = $500K = 50M tokens/year
- Governance burns: ~1M tokens/year (10 proposals executed)
- **Total**: ~110M tokens burned/year (11% of circulating supply)

**Deflation Effect**: Fixed 1B supply - 110M burned/year = Circulating supply shrinks, token value increases (assuming demand stays constant or grows).
