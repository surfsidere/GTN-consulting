## Four-Layer Token Utility

### Layer 1: Stake-for-Access (Primary Utility)
- NS stakes GTN for platform access (1-3 years locked)
- Three tiers sized for different NS scales
- Locked tokens = reduced circulating supply (price support)

### Layer 2: Governance Rights
- Progressive decentralization (founder → NS partners → full DAO)
- Weighted voting (Tier 3 = 5x votes)
- Governance scope: fees, treasury, upgrades, buybacks, incentives

### Layer 3: Network Incentives (Proof of Coordination)
- **Reward Pool**: 2% annual inflation (20M GTN/year)
- **Four Categories**:
  1. **Contribution Rewards** (50%): Earn GTN for assessments, intros, public goods
  2. **Quadratic Funding** (30%): Gitcoin-style grants for NS ecosystem
  3. **Cross-NS Collaboration** (15%): 2-5x multiplier for inter-NS projects (CREATIVE)
  4. **NS Growth Milestones** (5%): Bonus GTN for hitting targets (CREATIVE)

### Layer 4: Value Capture (Revenue Buybacks)
- **Model**: Braintrust-style (50% revenue → buy GTN from market)
- **Year 1**: $640K buyback budget → 6.4M GTN @ $0.10
- **Effect**: Constant buy pressure = price floor support
- **Governance**: Community votes burn vs. treasury

**Mitigation**:
- Pre-commit 2 Tier 3 NS BEFORE token launch
- "Founding NS" benefits: 50% discount Year 1, +20% bonus tokens
- Fallback: EVOLUTION.LIFE already $2M revenue (proven business)
- **Creative**: Price Protection Pool (10M GTN treasury allocation)

### Risk 2: Token Regulatory Scrutiny (15% probability)
**Mitigation**:
- MIDAO structure (non-US entity, reduces SEC jurisdiction)
- Clear utility: Stake-for-access = software license, NOT security
- NO public sale (only accredited investors)
- Howey Test: Fails "expectation of profit" test (staking for ACCESS)

### Risk 3: Token Price Crash (60% probability - crypto volatility)
**Mitigation**:
- Long lock-ups (1-3 years) = NS focus on utility, not speculation
- Revenue buybacks create price floor (Braintrust model)
- Even at 80% crash, still cheaper than traditional SaaS
- Staking rewards offset price drops

### Risk 4: Competing Platform (40% probability)
**Mitigation**:
- EVOLUTION.LIFE head start: 27K users, $2M revenue (proof)
- Network effects: More NS = more valuable
- Strategic NS partnerships (Próspera, Zuzalu) create lock-in
- Token alignment (competitors likely won't tokenize initially)

### Risk 5: NS Movement Fails (20% probability, highest impact)
**Mitigation**:
- GTN = coordination platform for ANY community (DAOs, coops, online communities)
- EVOLUTION.LIFE pivot ready (individual platform already works)
- Human coordination problems exist regardless of "network state" terminology
